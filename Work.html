My Work and Hobbies Schedule

Teacher

I worked as a New York City public school teacher until recently, notably March 2022. Teaching during the covid 19 outbreak since 2020 has tested the grip and commitment of many a one in the teaching world, just as it did in the healthcare sector. Still, it presented great opportunities as it led to educational stakeholders further developing and enhancing additional tools to keep teaching and to maximize student learning.

Personally, I enjoyed introducing new remote/digital teaching platforms to my students. Applications such as Edpuzzle, Nearpod, Paddlet, quizzlet, etc, helped make remote teaching via Zoom meetings or Microsoft Teams meetings a fun experience for my students.
