My Education

My educational journey took me through two continents and three countries.
I did my kindergarten years through secondary school in my beautiful home country, Burkina-Faso.
Then, went to college in Cameroon for my undergraduate studies in Theology. Next, I just completed
a Masters of Science degree in Education from Pace University in New York, and am currently pursuing
a Masters of Software Development from Boston University.

Pace University

Pace University is a private University having its main campus right at the end of the Brooklyn Bridge in
New York City. It was convenient to attend Pace given that I live nearby. However, being able to attend
Boston University remotely means that any location advantage that Pace University had is now non-existent.
